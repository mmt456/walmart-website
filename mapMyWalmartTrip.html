Walmart X Gracehopper: Project Innovation

Project "Map My Walmart Trip"


Project/Service Introduction:

Project "Map My Walmart Trip" targets customers who either want to make their trips to Walmart efficient, or have trouble finding items in walmart. "Map My Walmart Trip" can be implmented on the Walmart App with a tab. The app will show a general map of where you are in a particiapting Walmart store, and allow you to list items on your shopping list. Then, the app will map the customer through the most ideal route to pick up the items.

This service will create more efficient and less stressful trips to Walmart, improving their customer experience in participating stores. This app can also lesson the stress on employees so they can use their time to do something else than answer simple questions from customers.


The Problem:

Large stores like Walmart often change organization, and it can be difficult for customers to keep up. Particularly for those who don't go to Walmart often, who are tight on time, or just have difficulty finding items for any reason, shopping in stores like Walmart can be time consuming and stressful.


The Solution:

"Map My Walmart Trip" solves the listed problems byt utilizing shortest path algorithms such as Dijkstra's algorithm, making it easier for users to find their items and follow a route, similar to using a GPS. IT allows users to be efficient with their time and minimizes the stress that comes with not being able to find an item.
