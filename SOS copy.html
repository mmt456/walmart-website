Walmart X Gracehopper: Project Innovation

Project "SOS:" Save Our Shelves


Project/Service Introduction:

Project "Save Our Shelves," (or "SOS"), is an innovation that aids the workers at Walmart by alerting them when shelves in the region they are managing are low on stock. This projects makes use of sensors on shelves with uniformly packed items (the aisles sections), as well as connection to a worker's device the ping them with a notification.

This project will increase the efficiey of restocking shelves throughout the day. Particulary for items on sale or run out of stock frequently, this can also improve customer experience, as customers will not have to ask employees for more stock as often. This can also lead to decreases stress for the employee, as it helps them to better prioritize their time in telling them what shelves are most important to stock at the moment.


The Problem:

Many large stores with workers that are spread thin can run into problems with shelves not being stocked for long periods of time. Walmart is no exception to this. If shelves run out of stock during the day, it can cause customers to become upset and therefore damage their experience at Walmart.


The Solution

Project "SOS" allows workers to better prioritize which shelves to stock, so even if they are spread thin on some days, most shelves will be handled. Therefore, both workers and customers can be more at peace when it comes to finding a shelf with no items on it.
