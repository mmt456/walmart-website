Walmart X Gracehopper: Project Innovation

Project "Be My Guide"


Costs:

Participating Walmart stores will need to cover the cost of a couple extra employees, and the cost of training for them in working with people with differing abilities. Furthermore, there is cost of the development behind the technology that allows customers to request a guide, which will either through Walmart App on their phone, or verbally when they arrive at Walmart.


Benefits:

Being more accessible to more audiences allows Walmart to increase their profits made on in-store purchases. "Be My Guide" also promotes a more welcoming environment to their customers with disabilities. Overall, it makes the in-store Walmart experience more accessible, which can be beneficial to Walmart's reputation and improve customer experience.


Constraints:

There are constraints if the cost of implementing this innovation is too high. There are also constraints in the number of customers who can use a guide at a time. If there are not enough guides for customers at a given hour, some customer will be left with the challenge of navigating Walmart on their own. There is also the possibility of the guide no knowing the same language as the customer. Even if the guide is specifically hired knowing multiple languages, it can still be hard to accomodate for how many people there are in a community.
