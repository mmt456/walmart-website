Walmart X Gracehopper: Project Innovation

Project "Map My Walmart Trip"


Further Implemntation

the "Map My Walmart Trip" Project is still in the ideation phase, and therefore is far from being perfect. If Walmart decides to pick up this project, I reccommend a few further implementations, however furhter implementations are not limited to these few.


Include Feature on Shopping Carts

To increase accessibility to those without phones or would rather not use their phones, embedding hardware into some shopping carts could be a good alternative for them. However, this does increase the cost of development for the project.


Include a "Call for Help" Button

Including a button to call for help anywhere along your trip can alert a nearby worker your location. This can be helpful if an item has run out of stock, or the feature has routed the customer incorrectly. This may have constraints with customer privacy, however it does make calling for help easier than trying to find someone in the store.
